View our privacy policy here: Privacy Policy Connected Dreams Restore Effective Date: February 2026

Connected Dreams Restore (“the App”) respects your privacy and commits to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains data collection, data use, and your choices regarding your information.

Information We Collect
Health & Wellness Data The App may collect and locally store wellness-related information you enter or receive from connected devices including: -Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV) -Sleep timing and recovery metrics -Glucose readings (if entered manually) -Performance and wellness notes -Daily reflection entries

This data stores locally on your device unless you choose to export it.

Device Connectivity Data When connecting to Bluetooth-enabled wearable devices, the App may process: -Heart rate signals -Device connection status -Sensor quality indicators

This information supports app functionality.

Purchase Information If you subscribe to premium features, Apple processes payment information. The App does not receive or store your payment details.

How Your Information Gets Used
Your information: -displays recovery and wellness insights -supports wearable device connectivity -enables data export features -improves app functionality and stability

Data Storage
-Data stores locally on your device. -The App does not maintain a remote user account database. -Exported files save only when you choose to export them.

Data Sharing
Connected Dreams Restore does not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data. Data sharing only occurs: -if you choose to export it -if required by law

Third-Party Services
The App relies on Apple platform services for: -Bluetooth connectivity -In-App Purchases -System analytics and crash reporting (if enabled by Apple) -These services operate under Apple’s privacy policies.

Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Because data stores locally on your device, you control access through your device security settings.

Your Choices
-choose what data to enter -delete entries at any time -disconnect wearable devices -yearly subscriptions renew automatically unless you cancel in Apple ID account settings¹. -uninstall the App to remove all stored data

Children’s Privacy
This App is not directed to children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Medical Disclaimer
Connected Dreams Restore is designed for wellness and educational purposes only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.

Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may update periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
